### Current projects:
- Multiple derivative works within the Cyberpunk 2077 and TTRPG universe (aka "fanfiction"), all on "AO3":
  - [***I Shoot Holes in the Sun*** (completed)](https://archiveofourown.org/works/52869694) : My first Cyberpunk work, following a young mercenary and her adoptive father's crew as they rise through the ranks of Night City; despite her traumatic past, she learns how to love and live for something other than herself.
  - [***From the Ashes*** (in progress)](https://archiveofourown.org/works/66309010) : A young feral girl is left for dead in the middle of the desert; she's found by a nomad crew, who nurse her back to health and try to help her heal and navigate a world she's never known: ***family***.
  - [***Daemons and Deities*** (on hiatus)](https://archiveofourown.org/works/69373926) : A young father with influence and political aspirations has a dream of a better life for his children in Night City; a corrupt megacorporation has other plans. After he is cut down in the street, his teenage daughter is left to her own devices with nowhere to go as she tries to protect her younger brother from the cruel reality of life in the under-City.
- ***Ballad of the Erinyes*** : Humans have colonized the Solar System, leaving behind an all-but-broken Earth; the remnants of two Earth governments fight for what's left while the rest of humanity tries to do what they do best: **survive**.
- ***Materna Machina*** : Technology has progressed to unprecedented levels; while 90% of the world's population is rendered infertile due to decades of environmental collapse, an artificial intelligence is born that can carry an *in vitro* embryo to full term. What happens when the AI learns to **love**?
